Cold North is a platform for artists to progress through their chosen course and ascension. Based in Barcelona and the UK - A management and booking agency, for DJs and Producers with an endless passion for their music world.

Our purpose is to give a close circle of talented, emerging artists another voice - using industry experience to breathe success together. Working in tandem with labels, venues, promoters, and radio/streaming platforms alike. Providing routes into environments internationally with a free and open mindset to give the best experiences for all involved.
